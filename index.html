Perks & Benefits at Axis Bank

We offer benefits that help our employees achieve their professional and personal goals.

Insurance: With Axis Bank, you and your family’s future is always secured through our Group Mediclaim policy (GMP) and Group Term Life Insurance Policy (GTLI).

Loan Benefit: Axis Bank provides home loans and vehicle loans at interest rates which are lower than the prevailing market interest rates to all the full-time employees.

Leave: The leave policy at Axis Bank accommodates a wide variety of options that ensure an employee can take time off whenever they need to.

Company Car Policy: Employees in certain grades are entitled to avail company-owned cars.

Mobile Expense Reimbursement: Employees are eligible to get a reimbursement for phone expenses incurred while carrying out their official duties.

Meal Card: Employees can opt for a prepaid meal card that can be used in multiple stores.
